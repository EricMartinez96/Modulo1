Top Laner

A top laner in League of Legends is a player who controls the champion in the top lane, focusing on tankiness, dueling, and map pressure.

Jungler

A jungler in League of Legends is a player who navigates the jungle, clearing camps and providing support to lanes by ganking and controlling objectives.

Mid Laner

A mid laner in League of Legends is a player who takes the middle lane, typically playing champions with high burst damage or strong ability to roam and influence other lanes.

Attack Damage Carry (ADC)

An ADC (Attack Damage Carry) in League of Legends is a role played in the bot lane, focusing on dealing high sustained damage with ranged auto attacks. They are typically paired with a support and aim to farm gold, scale their damage output, and carry the team in the late game.

More Info
Support

A support in League of Legends is a role played in the bot lane, focusing on providing utility, protection, and crowd control for their ADC (Attack Damage Carry) during the laning phase and team fights. They assist their team by providing vision, healing, shielding, and initiating or disengaging fights to aid in their team's success.
