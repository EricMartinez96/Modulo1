Jarvan IV

Jarvan IV is back. Dominant, strong and determined on your solo queue.

Master of ganks and teamfight control, what more do you want to win a match? His good win rate is proof to that.

Amumu

The sad mummy with depression, after all, can not be dangerous, but not in this patch!

He is a great champion for those who like to initiate fights in an impressive way and deal high damage while maintaining solid durability. With Amumu, the definition of CC has a more threatening meaning.

Kha'Zix

Kha'Zix is still dominating the jungle!

Kha'Zix is a powerful assassin with strong potential for jungle combat and early duels. His constantly changing gameplay, thanks to evolution mechanics, allows for more decisions and a greater skill set. When deciding to play with this character, depending on the function, you should focus on both damage capability and survivability.
